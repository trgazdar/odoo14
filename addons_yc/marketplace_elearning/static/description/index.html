e-Learning: Breaking Barriers to Study

Many people cannot take time to go out for their studies; they always seek out the solution to learn from home. Going out to study can take extra time as well as effort.

Moreover, everybody is going through a pandemic of Covid19 in 2020. Hence, there is a situation of lockdown all around the world. So, E-learning is the apt solution to break the barriers for the people to study.

The global e-learning is projected to surpass 243 billion U.S. dollars at a 5.08 percent CAGR in 2022.

Nowadays, e-learning is not an option but has become a lifestyle for the students.

Invite Others to Post their Courses Online

Like a coaching centre, you can build an environment for all different students to study in Odoo.

Odoo Marketplace eLearning lets you launch an online school for learning; so that students can pay for the courses they want to study and can continue with flexibility.

You can invite teachers from any corner of the world to upload their courses online. The enrolled student can opt for the courses he/she is interested in.

Maintain the Quality of your Online School

You can maintain the Quality of your Online School; simply by approving the courses which are worth for the students.

Hence, the module gives you the ability to approve or reject any course so that helps you to maintain the quality of your online school.

Detailed Feature List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Odoo Marketplace eLearning Module

Launch your Own Online School

  • You can launch your online classes in Odoo.
  • Invite teachers to sell their courses on Odoo Marketplace.

Teachers Can Add Their Courses

  • As sellers in Marketplace, teachers can create service type products.
  • Then, they can associate courses to that product.
  • Moreover, teachers can add the course charges in the serviceable product.

Approve/Disapprove The Courses

  • As an admin, you can approve the apt courses
  • You can also disapprove the courses by other teaches which you may not find apt

Students can easily Study Online

  • Students can pay online and study the course as per their wish.

Quizzes for Interactive learning

  • You can add quizzes in each lesson.
  • You can add Reward points for the quiz as per the number of attempts.

  • Student View
